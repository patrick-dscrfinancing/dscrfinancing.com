FAQ

What is a DSCR loan?

A DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) loan is based on property income vs debt, instead of personal income verification.

Who is it for?

Real estate investors buying or refinancing rentals — including entrepreneurs and self-employed borrowers.

Do you finance short-term rentals?

Yes. We can structure DSCR for Airbnb/STR lanes when the property and lender rules fit.

What states do you lend in?

DSCR programs can be offered across many states (licensing and program rules apply).
